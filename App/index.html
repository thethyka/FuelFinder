Fuel Finder
Route-aware fuel cost optimiser · Hackathon project

FuelFinder finds the cheapest fuel stop along your drive — not just by price, but by true total cost. It fetches live prices from the WA Government FuelWatch feed, then models the fuel burned reaching each station and the detour distance, recommending the single stop that minimises your trip cost.

How to use
  1. Draw your route using the directions panel on the right
  2. Enter your fuel efficiency (L/100km), desired fill amount, and current tank level
  3. Tick any loyalty cards you hold for automatic discounts
  4. Click Calculate — the optimal stop is pinned to your route
